# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

The FireWatch community is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone who participates, regardless of background
or identity.

## Our standard

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][covenant]** as its Code
of Conduct. Please read it in full at the link below — it defines the behavior we expect
of all contributors and maintainers, and the behavior the project will not tolerate.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Enforcement

Concerns may be reported confidentially to the project maintainers at
**galiperdem@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated
promptly and fairly, and maintainers will respect the privacy and security of the
reporter of any incident.
